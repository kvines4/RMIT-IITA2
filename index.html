The necessary skills for each of these jobs:

Information Security Consultant

Information Security Consultants are IT professionals who are responsible for implementing cybersecurity solutions to fulfil the needs of an organisation.

  • Have a solid professional network based on good relationships and reputation
  • Strong focus on business development
  • Experience with risk management principles including risk assessment methodologies
  • Good working knowledge of relevant standards, security frameworks and regulations such as ISO 27001, NIST, CSF, Essential 8, PCI DSS, VPDSS, CPS 234, AESCSF, ASD ISM & PSPF
  • 10+ years of consulting experience in information security, ideally combined with a strong technical background based on a tertiary degree or work experience
  • Understand business drivers and context that shape cyber security requirements
  • Able to engage with stakeholders from all areas/levels of an organisation, including executive and board level
  • Experience with security policy frameworks and policies, including the implementation of these in an organisation
  • Have built a cyber security practice from the ground up before would be of an advantage
  • Previous experience working in a large and complex organisation
  • Experience working as a Business Analyst Process Consultant,
  • Knowledge and experience in Security and ISO Best practice
  • Ability to influence and work with Senior executives

Senior Software Engineer

Senior software engineers are highly experienced software engineers whodevelop information systems by being able to resolve complex software issues, understand and buildarchitecture, and can lead software engineering teams.

  • Application development
  • Experience in Low Latency and High Frequency Environments (system needs to be able to process high
  • amounts of data in milliseconds)
  • Embedded OS experience and distributed systems
  • JVM languages (Scala is desirable)
  • Low level platform bring-up and device drivers

Solutions Engineer

Solutions engineers are IT professionals who are the interfaces between the business and clients. They have both technology and business skills to help communication between the two parties and implement solutions.

  • Technical background and experience with Java, Kubernetes, Dockers, React
  • Agile methodologies with scrum, unit testing and code reviews
  • Cloud infrastructure management
  • Managing and adhering cybersecurity to policies and identifying errors
  • Incident response for ensuring platform remaining in operational state

IT Operations Manager

IT operations managers maintain control over IT services and oversee the underlying network infrastructure and systems functionality.

  • 10+ years of experience in IT Support and Infrastructure across multiple locations
  • 3-5 years of management
  • Experience with software as a service (SaaS)
  • Experience managing hundreds of user accounts with interconnection and federation on a national/global scale
  • Complex IT project management
  • Excellent communication skills both written and verbal
  • Ability to communicate with people across all levels
  • Enthusiasm for learning new skills
  • Ability to adjust quickly to changing priorities and make quick decisions with limited information
  • Effective organizational, communication, leadership, and teamwork skills
  • Passion for new technology and commitment to learning new skills
