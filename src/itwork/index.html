Description of interview

Interview was conducted between Logan and Matthew where they discussed what it's like to work in the IT industry, what interactions he undertakes with other professionals, what challenges he faces within the industry and how he entered the field. Video and transcript are attached in the appendix.

Interview Reflection

Overall, the interview went very well. There was a good back and forth and the topics covered were very illuminating as now I have an insight into what it's like to be an IT professional. There were multiple things I took away from the interview. The biggest would be having to be able to work with a team as you are rarely working on a project yourself and that you need to constantly be learning and getting to know new technologies. In moving forward into the IT industry, I will have to get better at constantly learning and growing as well as developing my teamwork skills.
