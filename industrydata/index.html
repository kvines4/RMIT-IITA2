A strength of the team is its design and front-end development skills. There are many programming languages and specialised skills for them to learn if they want to attain senior positions in the industry. The top three in-demand IT-specific skills include SQL, JavaScript, and JAVA. However, JAVA skills are stronger than those of JavaScript and SQL. The three highest-ranked IT-specific skills not in the required set include business management, building relationships, and SAP.

Overall, the team ranks solidly in general skills. Communication skills are the most highly ranked of all the skills, and the team has excellent written and verbal communication skills. Problem-solving, organisation skills, and writing are also skills available to the team. The three highest-ranked general skills not in the required skill set are troubleshooting, planning, and detail orientation. The higher degree of introversion and independence among some team members may make collaboration difficult, although strong communication skills could mitigate this.

The Burning Glass data does not change my opinion on the ideal jobs for the team members because they all express a high motivation to learn and have a quick ability to do so. Even if some of the skills required are lacking now, they can quickly learn what exactly it is that they need to do and use their general skills to acquire more specialised knowledge. A major hindrance is that they may lack a growth mindset and give up on their attempts to reach their goals.
